DMCA Policy

This page describes how we handle copyright-related notices.

1. What to include

Please include the URL(s) of the content, proof of ownership/authorization, your contact information, and a clear description of the claim.

2. Process

We will review complete requests and take appropriate action within a reasonable time.

3. Contact

Please use the Contact page.
