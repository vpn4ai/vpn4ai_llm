StrongVPN Review: Old-school, conservative, and “quiet”

Why we tested StrongVPN

StrongVPN is a long-running provider with a reputation for being stable and straightforward. It’s often described as “does the job” rather than “feature-packed”.

Usability

The client feels traditional. There are no major weaknesses, but also fewer standout features. The overall experience is: it won’t bother you much.

Performance in varied network conditions

In more complex conditions, stability and recovery may not be as strong as the most mainstream providers. It can be more suitable in relatively relaxed conditions.

Who StrongVPN is best for

  • Users who prefer a traditional VPN experience
  • Users who don’t need many new features

Who might want to look elsewhere

  • Users who require stronger stability and recovery in complex conditions
