Ivacy VPN Review: Budget-friendly, but don’t expect top-tier stability

Why Ivacy is included

Ivacy often shows up in “cheap VPN” lists, especially with long-term discounts. We tested it with a simple goal: how usable is it at a low price point?

Onboarding & first-time feel

The client is traditional and functional. Setup and connection are straightforward, but the overall polish and “it just works” factor is closer to a budget product.

Performance in varied network conditions

In practice, stability depends a lot on which server you choose and the time of day. During our use, it behaves more like a backup option than a primary daily VPN.

Scenario notes

General browsing

Works for basic access, but may require switching servers more often.

Streaming

Streaming “support” is inconsistent in real use. If streaming is your main goal, you’ll likely want a more reliable provider.

Pros & trade-offs

Pros:

  • Very low long-term pricing during promos
  • Core VPN features are present

Trade-offs:

  • Stability is inconsistent
  • Not ideal as a long-term primary VPN

Who Ivacy is best for

  • Very budget-sensitive users
  • Backup / secondary usage
